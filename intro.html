About Me

Hi there! My name is Edward Ryan, and I am a software developer with expertise in front-end and back-end programming languages, as well as database management. I am skilled in creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites, as well as developing customized solutions that meet specific client requirements.

In my most recent role as a Freelance Front-End Developer, I collaborated with clients to identify their needs and delivered responsive and accessible static websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I also conducted cross-browser testing through Git/GitHub and optimized website performance through CSS frameworks and JavaScript libraries.

In my previous role as a Teaching Assistant, I assisted in teaching undergraduate physics courses by conducting laboratory sections. I also completed a Master of Science in Physics at the University of Surrey, where I worked on scientific research projects such as Astrophysical Research using Imaging, Spectroscopy, and General Relativity and Simulation Analysis of a Periodically Structured Dielectric Material.

Outside of my professional career, I enjoy playing baseball and managing a team of athletes as a Head Coach/Manager. I am also an avid video game player, and I love watching movies and shows (excluding horror genre). My favorite games are Rocket League and MLB the Show.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me. Please feel free to check out my GitHub profile to view some of my work.
